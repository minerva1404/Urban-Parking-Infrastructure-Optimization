## Power BI Dashboard Summary

- Built an interactive Power BI dashboard on top of the Gold layer to visualize car park infrastructure and operational insights.
- Highlights key KPIs including total car parks, average infrastructure score, average decks per car park, night parking activity, and data freshness.
- Uses spatial and distribution visuals to compare infrastructure quality across car park types and parking systems.
- Provides a clear view of night parking trends and infrastructure density to aid decision-making.
- Enables stakeholders to quickly identify areas for improvement and plan resource allocation efficiently.
- Translates streaming analytics into actionable, business-ready insights in a visually engaging way.
